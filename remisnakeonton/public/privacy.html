PRIVACY POLICY

1. Introduction

Snake.io TON ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Telegram Mini App and the Snake.io TON game (collectively, the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Telegram User Information

When you access our app through Telegram, we receive:

  • Telegram user ID
  • Username (if available)
  • Display name
  • Profile photo URL (if available)
  • Language preference
  • Platform information (iOS, Android, Web)

2.2 Wallet Information

When you connect a TON wallet, we store:

  • Wallet address (public key)
  • Transaction history within the app
  • Purchase records

2.3 Game Data

We automatically collect:

  • Game scores and statistics
  • Virtual currency balance (Gold)
  • Unlocked items and collectibles
  • Level and XP progression
  • Match history and performance metrics

2.4 Analytics Data

We use Telegram Mini Apps Analytics SDK to collect:

  • App usage patterns
  • Session duration
  • Feature engagement
  • Device information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the Service
  • Processing in-app purchases and transactions
  • Saving your game progress and preferences
  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve the game
  • Preventing fraud and cheating
  • Communicating with you about updates and features
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using:

  • Local Storage: Game progress saved locally on your device
  • Blockchain: Transaction records permanently stored on TON Blockchain
  • Secure Servers: User profiles and analytics data encrypted in transit and at rest

While we implement security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with:

  • Telegram Messenger: For user authentication and app delivery
  • TON Blockchain: For cryptocurrency transactions via TON Connect
  • Telegram Mini Apps Analytics: For usage analytics

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing your information. We encourage you to review them.

6. Blockchain Transparency

IMPORTANT: All TON blockchain transactions are public and immutable. When you make a purchase or withdrawal:

  • Transaction details are permanently recorded on the public blockchain
  • Your wallet address is visible to anyone
  • Transaction amounts and timestamps are publicly accessible
  • These records cannot be deleted or modified

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (where applicable)
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Disconnect your TON wallet at any time

Note: Blockchain transaction records cannot be deleted due to the immutable nature of distributed ledgers.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification through Telegram (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

11. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing your information includes:

  • Performance of contract (providing the game service)
  • Legitimate interests (improving the game, preventing fraud)
  • Consent (where you have provided it)
  • Legal obligations (compliance with laws)
